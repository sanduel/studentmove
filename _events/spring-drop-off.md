Bring your trash, recycling, and reusable items to the supervised drop-off site. Open 10 AM to 3 PM on May 1, 3, 4, 5, and 6.

Reusable items in good condition will be sorted and distributed to local nonprofits. Trash and recycling will be properly disposed of.

No on-site assistance for unloading is available. Large materials must be pre-sized to fit in dumpsters.
